Pesto

Description

Pesto or more fully pesto alla genovese is a paste made of crushed garlic, pine nuts, salt, basil leaves, grated cheese such as Parmesan or pecorino sardo, and olive oil. It originated in the Italian city of Genoa, and is used to dress pasta and sometimes soups.

Ingredients

  • 60g Basil
  • 2.5g salt
  • 30g pine nuts
  • zest of half a small lemon
  • 10g lemon juice
  • 1 snall garlic clove
  • 15g parmigiano reggiano
  • 54g extra virgin olive oil (EVOO)
  • 28g butter

Steps

  1. add basil, salt, nuts, lemon zest and juice, garlic, parm, butter and EVOO to a blender and blend until smooth and relatively homogeneous.
  2. Enjoy on pasta, bread, in marinades and all other kinds of applications.
