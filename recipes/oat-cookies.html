Oat Cookies

Description

a basic cookie containing rolled oats

Ingredients

  • 90g flour
  • 0.8g salt
  • 3g baking soda
  • 115g butter
  • 150g brown suger
  • 25g of egg about half a large egg beaten
  • 5g vanilla extract
  • 120g oats

Steps

  1. combine brown suger and butter into a bowl and cream them together with a sturdy spoon
  2. add egg and vanilla to suger mixture mix well
  3. add flour, salt and baking soda ontop of mixture use a fork or whisk to mix the dry ingredients together then mix the dry ingredients with the wet
  4. mix oats into the batter and form into 40g balls
  5. place balls on baking sheet and bake at 350 F for 9-11 minutes
  6. let cookies cool for a few minutes then transfer to a cooling rack
  7. Enjoy
